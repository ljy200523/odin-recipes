Avocado Milkshake

Homepage

Description

An Avocado milkshake is a creamy, nutritious, and refreshing beverage popular in Southeast Asia, Brazil, and Morocco, made by blending ripe avocado with milk and a sweetener. The avocado's healthy fats provide a rich, smooth, and filling texture, often without needing ice cream

The avocado milkshake is highly versatile and can be customized to suit personal preferences.
For a thicker shake: Add more avocado, a frozen banana, or a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
For a thinner consistency: Add more milk until the desired texture is reached.
Flavor enhancements: Incorporate ingredients like cocoa powder for a chocolate avocado milkshake, a squeeze of fresh lime juice to brighten the flavor and prevent browning, or warm spices like cinnamon.
Nutritional boost: Add chia seeds, protein powder, or a handful of spinach for extra nutrients.
Vegan option: Use plant-based milk (almond, soy, or coconut milk) instead of dairy milk.
Southeast Asian style: Use gula melaka (palm sugar) or sweetened condensed milk as the sweetener.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups milk
  • 1 avocado, peeled and pitted
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 cup ice cubes - optional for a colder shake
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract - optional

Steps

  1. Prepare the avocado: Slice the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a blender.
  2. Add ingredients: Pour in the milk, sweetener, and vanilla extract (if using). Add ice cubes if you desire a chilled drink.
  3. Blend: Blend on a medium to high speed until the mixture is completely smooth and creamy.
  4. Serve immediately: Pour into glasses and enjoy right away, as the avocado can oxidize and turn brown over time.
